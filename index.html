Background

I'm currently a Ph.D. candidate in the Nuclear Engineering and Radiological Sciences program at the University of Michigan. In brief, I study computational methods for radiation transport. More specifically, I'm investigating hybrid (stochastic/deterministic) methods for transient reactor calculations with multi-level physics approximations and parallel-in-time integration techniques. I previously completed a B.S. in Nuclear Engineering with a minor in Material Science and Engineering at Texas A&M University. Additionally, I've been fortunate enough to spend a few summers interning at various national laboratories, where I hope to establish my career some day.
